# Planning Document for Website ## Website Goal The goal of the website is to provide a comprehensive platform for users to access resources and information effectively while ensuring ease of navigation and a user-friendly experience. ## Success Evaluation Success will be evaluated based on user engagement metrics, feedback through surveys, and the achievement of predefined targets such as user registrations and content accessibility. ## Target Audience The primary target audience includes students and educators seeking accessible educational resources, as well as general users interested in up-to-date information on various topics. ## Key Design Considerations - **Accessibility**: Ensure the website is usable for individuals with disabilities. - **Responsiveness**: The design must adapt to different screen sizes and devices. - **Navigation**: Create a clear and intuitive navigation system to enhance user experience. ## Home Page Wireframe Section - Include a header with the site logo and main navigation links. - A prominent search bar to facilitate content discovery. - Featured content area that showcases important updates or resources. - A footer with links to policies and contact information. ## Site Map Section - Overview of the site's structure, including main sections, sub-sections, and links to key pages to help users find information easily. ## Rationale These considerations are designed to create a user-centric platform that not only meets the needs of its audience but also encourages repeated use and further exploration of content within the site.
